About Me


Welcome and thank you for visiting my site! This is my first personal webpage that I coded from scratch. With this side project, I thought it would be fun to introduce you to a few of my favorite things. First and foremost, I have three very naughty but also very loving and spunky dogs name Tobey, Jamey, and Otis. I share the glorious responsibility of caring for them with my favorite person in the whole world, my partner and best friend, Kiel. I am a huge lover of wine and one can say I'm obsessed - though I am by no means a wine expert (or snob). I'm an ardent foodie and enjoy learning about cuisines from around the world. I'm addicted to food travel, so when I have free time you can be sure to find me immersed in such documentaries, books or travelling to personally learn and experience regional food and cultures. I'm also very passionate about sustainability and am continually working to reduce waste in my everyday life. I make a conscious effort to support local businesses and sustainable companies with my spending power and urge everyone to do the same.
